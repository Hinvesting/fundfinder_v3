## How to Search for Funding

FundFinder uses advanced AI to find funding opportunities that match your specific needs and location.

### Step 1: Enter Your Location

Be as specific as possible for best results:
- ‚úÖ **Good**: "Seattle, Washington"
- ‚úÖ **Better**: "King County, Washington"
- ‚ùå **Too vague**: "USA"

**Why location matters**: Our AI prioritizes local opportunities first:
1. **City-level grants** (e.g., Seattle Small Business Grant)
2. **County programs** (e.g., King County Innovation Fund)
3. **State initiatives** (e.g., Washington State Loans)
4. **National programs** (only if specific to your industry)

### Step 2: Describe Your Business Type

Tell us what kind of business you have:
- "Tech Startup"
- "Restaurant"
- "Retail Store"
- "Non-profit Organization"
- "Manufacturing Company"
- "Service Business"

**Pro Tip**: Be specific! "Sustainable fashion startup" gives better results than just "startup".

### Step 3: Explain Your Funding Purpose

What do you need funding for?
- Equipment purchases
- Business expansion
- Working capital
- Inventory
- Marketing campaigns
- Hiring employees
- Research & development

**Example**: "Need $10,000 for commercial kitchen equipment to expand catering services"

### Step 4: Get Your Results

Click **"Find Funding with AI"** and wait 5-10 seconds.

You'll receive **3 personalized funding opportunities**, each showing:
- **Name**: The grant, loan, or investor program
- **Type**: Grant, Loan, or Investor
- **Amount**: Funding range available
- **Deadline**: When to apply by
- **Link**: Direct application URL
- **Match Reason**: Why this is perfect for you

## Saving Opportunities

### How to Save

After receiving search results, you can save opportunities to review later:

1. Click the **"Save"** button next to any funding opportunity
2. The item is added to your "Saved Items" list
3. Access saved items anytime from your dashboard

### View Saved Items

1. Click **"My Saved Items"** in the navigation
2. See all your saved opportunities in one place
3. Each item shows:
   - When you saved it
   - All the original details (amount, deadline, link, etc.)
   - The match reason

### Organize Your Applications

Use saved items to:
- **Track progress**: Which ones you've applied to
- **Compare options**: See all opportunities side-by-side
- **Stay organized**: Never lose a funding opportunity
- **Plan timeline**: Sort by deadlines

## FAQ

### How does FundFinder find opportunities?

We use Google's Gemini AI, trained to understand:
- Geographic funding landscapes
- Business type requirements
- Eligibility criteria
- Application processes

The AI analyzes millions of funding sources and matches them to your specific situation.

### Are these real funding opportunities?

Yes! All results are real grants, loans, or investor programs. However, always verify:
- Current availability
- Exact eligibility requirements
- Application deadlines
- Amount ranges

FundFinder provides leads; you complete the applications.

### Why only 3 results per search?

Quality over quantity. We show the **3 best matches** for your specific needs rather than overwhelming you with dozens of mediocre options.

### Can I search for someone else?

Yes! Just enter their:
- Business location
- Business type
- Funding needs

Great for consultants, advisors, or business partners.

### Do I need to be in the US?

Currently, FundFinder focuses primarily on US-based opportunities. International expansion coming soon!

### What if I don't find a match?

Try:
1. **Broaden location**: County instead of city
2. **Adjust business type**: Be more general
3. **Change purpose**: Different funding needs
4. **Search again tomorrow**: New opportunities added regularly

### Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use **Stripe**, the same payment processor used by Amazon, Shopify, and Google. We never store your credit card information.

### Can I manage my subscription?

Yes! Pro users can access the **Customer Portal** anytime:

1. Click **"Manage Subscription"** button (appears after upgrading)
2. You'll be redirected to the secure Stripe portal
3. From there you can:
   - View payment history and invoices
   - Update payment method
   - Download receipts

**Note**: Pro is a one-time payment (not recurring), so there are no recurring charges to manage.

### Can I get a refund?

Pro subscriptions are one-time payments with instant access. Contact support@fundfinder.com if you have concerns within 7 days of purchase.

### What happens to my saved items if I don't upgrade?

Your saved items are yours forever, regardless of plan. Free users can save unlimited opportunities.

### How do I cancel my account?

Contact support@fundfinder.com. Note: Pro is a one-time payment, not a subscription.
